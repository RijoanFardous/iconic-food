About Us

One of the most difficult things I’ve found when trying to follow a healthy lifestyle is eating out at restaurants. When you work so hard at home to follow healthy eating guidelines, eating at a restaurant can feel like a step backwards.
At restaurants, you can say goodbye to knowing everything that goes into your food and, most likely, to avoiding unhealthy ingredients, like butter, salt, and lots of saturated fats.
